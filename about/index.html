Office Admin to Coding Queen

My Journey into Tech

Throughout my career, I have had the same experience in every job: within months of starting, I would far exceed the expectations of my employers. They would be amazed at how organized I was, or a new, more efficient process I had designed. To them, it was miraculous. To me, it was tech. Finding new tools and technology to improve any task helped me to succeed time and time again. But I thought that was all technology would ever be to me – a tool that I used, not built.

That perception changed when I joined a remarkable FinTech company. I was surrounded by intelligent and talented engineers who were building incredible products, and I felt a spark ignite within me. When a friend and colleague enrolled in a coding boot camp, I was inspired by her bravery and decided to take the leap myself.

From the very beginning, I realized that I had found my true calling. Coding tapped into my creative mind and allowed me to channel my passion for problem-solving. I discovered that I possessed a natural aptitude for it and found joy in the process of building tools and applications from scratch.

My journey to transition from administrative roles to software engineering has been an eye-opening experience. I no longer see technology as just a tool; I am now an active participant in its creation. I have learned the importance of embracing new opportunities and stepping outside of my comfort zone. Not only has embracing this new career path allowed me to develop a new level of technical expertise, but it has also instilled in me a newfound sense of confidence in my own ability to learn and grow and succeed.
